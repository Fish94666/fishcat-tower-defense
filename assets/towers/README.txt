Put your custom tower PNG files here.

Required filenames:
- basic.png
- sniper.png

Notes:
- Transparent backgrounds work best.
- `basic.png` is scaled proportionally to fit within 42x48.
- `sniper.png` is scaled proportionally to fit within 64x60.
- The renderer assumes the left side of the image is the muzzle/front.
- The renderer mirrors the image horizontally on right-facing angles to keep the sprite upright.
- If the image is missing or fails to load, the game falls back to the simple circle tower.
